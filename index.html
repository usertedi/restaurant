About Us

Welcome to Gebeta Restaurant! We celebrate the flavors and culture of Ethiopia with every dish we serve. Founded in 2020 in the heart of Addis Ababa, our mission is to offer an authentic and memorable dining experience for locals and visitors alike. From traditional injera to spicy tibs and coffee ceremony, our food brings people together like a true gebeta.
